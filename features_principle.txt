The user has to find a real estate ad' URL on Leboncoin.fr.
Then the user can copy and paste this URL in the field text.
He has to click on the compute button.
Most of the work is done on a background basis.
The App is finally showing some information concerning the real estate ad the user found.
The App is especially furnishing an overall advice concerning the price of the real estate ad
(good or bad price concerning the price per square meter). The App could also show other
information such as the minimum price per square meter in this area and the maximum price 
per square meter in this area.
